Stoker Class

They are known to be the most dangerous fire-breathing dragons. Some of them, such as the Monstrous Nightmare are known for their ability to set themselves on fire. They have a high shot limit, however the more tired they are, the weaker the less likely they are to produce flames. The well-known dragons of this class are the Monstrous Nightmare, Terrible Terror, and the Singetail.
